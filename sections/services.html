Personal Estate Planning

Tokenized Asset Collective provides a secure, automated framework for ensuring your digital assets transfer exactly as intended — without intermediaries or uncertainty. Starting with digital assets, our approach lays the foundation for a programmable, trust-based digital estate system that can extend to any tokenized or real-world asset.

The Framework

Our step-by-step model turns complex inheritance processes into an automated, verifiable on-chain experience.

1

Establish the Trust

Create a digital trust that securely holds your digital assets and defines precise inheritance conditions. This becomes the foundation of your estate.

2

Prepare Beneficiaries

Each heir sets up a verified wallet, ensuring secure and direct ownership transfer when conditions are met.

3

Tokenize Ownership

The trust issues tokens that represent ownership stakes in the estate — programmable, divisible, and fully transparent.

4

Automated Distribution

Upon the verified passing of the settlor, tokens are distributed automatically to each heir’s wallet.
