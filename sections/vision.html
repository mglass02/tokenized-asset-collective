Our Vision

Founded on a single belief — that the digital wealth we build should never fade with time.

Where It All Began

It started with a question: “What happens to our digital assets when we’re gone?” In an age of tokenized wealth, there was no trusted bridge ensuring that on-chain assets reached their rightful heirs. TAC was created to change that.

What We’re Building Today

We’re developing on-chain estate automation — a trustless system that ensures cryptocurrencies, NFTs, and tokenized assets are transferred securely to beneficiaries, without intermediaries or loss of control.

The Future: Bridging Digital and Real Worlds

Beyond blockchain assets, we’re building frameworks to include Real World Assets (RWAs) — real estate, equity, and tangible holdings — enabling unified estate management across digital and physical domains.

Our Mission

To establish the world’s first programmable inheritance infrastructure — securing every form of value and ensuring what we create today finds its rightful place tomorrow.
